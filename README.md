# MediSync
A Digital Health Record System developed for the Google Cloud Hack Sprint Hackathon

## 📖 Project Overview

MediSync is a secure Digital Health Record System designed to help patients store, manage, and share their medical records digitally. The system aims to reduce paperwork, improve accessibility, and enable healthcare professionals to access patient information more efficiently.

## ❗ Problem Statement

Many hospitals still rely on paper-based medical records, making it difficult for patients to access and manage their health information. Medical reports can be lost or damaged, and patients often need to carry physical documents to every hospital visit. This leads to delays, repeated tests, and inconvenience for both patients and healthcare providers.

## 💡 Proposed Solution

MediSync provides a secure digital platform where patients can store, organize, and access their medical records anytime and anywhere. The system allows users to upload prescriptions, laboratory reports, and other health documents while enabling authorized healthcare professionals to access them securely when required.

## ✨ Features
- 🔐 Secure User Login
- 👤 Patient Profile Management
- 📄 Upload Medical Records
- 📂 View Medical History
- 💊 Prescription Management
- 🔍 Easy Search for Records
- ☁️ Secure Cloud Storage
- 👨‍⚕️ Secure Record Sharing with Doctors
