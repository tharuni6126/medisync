# 📄 MediSync Project Report

## 1. Project Title
MediSync – Digital Health Record System

---

## 2. Abstract

MediSync is a secure cloud-based Digital Health Record System developed for the Google Cloud Hack Sprint Hackathon. It enables patients to securely store, manage, and share their medical records while allowing authorized healthcare professionals to access patient information efficiently.

---

## 3. Problem Statement

Hospitals still depend on paper-based records. This causes delays, misplaced documents, repeated medical tests, and inconvenience for both patients and doctors.

---

## 4. Objectives

- Digitize medical records
- Reduce paperwork
- Secure patient information
- Enable faster healthcare services
- Improve accessibility

---

## 5. Proposed Solution

Develop a secure web platform where patients can upload, manage, and share their medical records. Doctors can securely access patient records after authorization.

---

## 6. Key Features

- Secure Login
- Patient Dashboard
- Upload Reports
- Medical History
- Prescription Management
- Search Records
- Google Cloud Storage
